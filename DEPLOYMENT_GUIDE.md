# Taste with Taka Website - Custom Domain Setup Guide

## 📋 What You Have
Your complete website consists of these 3 files:
- `index.html` - Your main website file
- `styles.css` - All styling and design
- `script.js` - Interactive features and animations
- Plus your image folder: `user_input_files/` with all your photos

## 🌐 How to Connect Your Own Domain & Remove Watermark

### Option 1: Popular Hosting Providers (Recommended)

**1. Choose a hosting provider:**
- **Netlify** (Free tier available) - Easiest for beginners
- **Vercel** (Free tier available) - Great performance
- **GitHub Pages** (Free) - If you have GitHub account
- **Hostinger** (Paid, ~$3/month) - Professional hosting
- **Bluehost** (Paid, ~$5/month) - Full-featured hosting

**2. Purchase your domain:**
- Use **Namecheap**, **GoDaddy**, or **Google Domains**
- Popular domain extensions: `.com`, `.net`, `.org`
- Example: `tastewithtaka.com`

**3. Upload your files:**
- Create account with hosting provider
- Upload all 4 items (3 files + image folder)
- Point your domain to the hosting

### Option 2: Detailed Step-by-Step (Netlify - Free)

**Step 1: Get Your Files Ready**
- Download all files from this chat
- Keep the folder structure intact

**Step 2: Create Netlify Account**
1. Go to [netlify.com](https://netlify.com)
2. Sign up for free account
3. Click "Deploy to Netlify"

**Step 3: Upload Your Website**
1. Drag and drop your website folder to Netlify
2. Your site will get a random URL like `amazing-site-123.netlify.app`
3. This removes the watermark automatically!

**Step 4: Connect Your Custom Domain**
1. Buy domain from registrar (Namecheap, GoDaddy, etc.)
2. In Netlify dashboard: Go to "Domain Settings"
3. Click "Add custom domain"
4. Enter your domain name
5. Follow DNS setup instructions
6. Wait 24-48 hours for propagation
